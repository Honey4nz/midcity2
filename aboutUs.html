When it comes to finding a dependable auto mechanic in Wellington, look no further than Mid City Auto Repairs.
Initially located in Wellington central and owned by Clive Rowley (who still works with us today) Mid City Auto Repairs has been operating for 40+ years.
In 2020, we decided to move out of Wellington city to Petone as many of the industrial companies were moving away from the city centre to make space for residential apartments. It was also an opportunity to be closer to our suppliers and to have more businesses we could refer to.
In the same year, the business changed hands to myself, Jujhar Signh Dhillon. I had worked at Mid City Auto Repairs for 12 years; I always wanted to take over a workshop or to start my own business and looked forward to this new venture.
Fast forward to present day and we have a well-oiled auto shop, a great team of mechanics behind us, and customers who rave about our service and have been doing business with us for 40 years.
Don’t settle for second best when it comes to your vehicle. Mid City Auto Repairs offers you the highest standard in automotive repair services

OUR VALUES

HONEST & UPFRONT APPROACH

There are no surprises with Mid City Auto Repairs! Our team will provide you with the full details of all work being done on your vehicle. You will know exactly what is being done and how much it will cost. If you have any questions before, during, or after the repairs, our friendly staff will be more than happy to answer them.

QUALITY WORKMANSHIP

Here at Mid City Auto Repairs, we pride ourselves on our workmanship. Whatever the job, we ensure the utmost care is taken to best serve your vehicle. Our staff are extremely skilled in all aspects of their job, ensuring you have total peace of mind when dropping your car off to us.

FAIR PRICING

We provide fair pricing for every single job. Mid City Auto Repairs prides itself on building excellent relationships with all of our clients so we are the go-to mechanic for all your vehicle needs. Ensuring you feel like you’ve been charged fairly for the work done is one of the many ways we achieve this.
