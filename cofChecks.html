CERTIFICATE OF FITNESS

Certificate of Fitness (CoF) for Your Car

We also provide a Certificate of Fitness for your car. A certificate of fitness (CoF) is a regular check to ensure that your vehicle meets the required safety standards. Vehicles requiring this certification are all passenger service vehicles such as Ubers and taxis and rental vehicles. A Certificate of Fitness is similar to a Warrant of Fitness – it ensures your car is fully functional, roadworthy and safe for passengers.
