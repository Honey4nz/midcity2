OUR SERVICES

We provide a variety of services to both company and personal cars. At Mid City Auto Repairs, no job is too small and we take pride in every job that we do. Our open, honest communication and fair pricing means that we’ll only recommend fixes and repairs that are necessary to ensure your car is roadworthy and safe to drive.

WOF CHECKS

At Mid City Auto Repairs, we take our WOF service seriously and provide a full report of anything that needs to be repaired or replaced. Give us the go-ahead and we’ll take care of any issues too. That way, you can be confident your car is compliant and safe to drive.

REPAIRS

Whatever it is that your vehicle needs, our automotive mechanics can get it done right! You can depend on our team to change your oil, transmission fluid or brake fluid, update your WOF or carry out any auto electrical repairs.

BRAKES

Noisey, smoking or faulty brakes? Worn brake pads and other brake issues can fail on a Warrant of Fitness or can even cause an accident. Don’t take risks with your safety – get your brakes inspected before something serious happens on the road.

SERVICING

Our vehicle servicing is designed to ensure we stay on top of the key maintenance areas of your vehicle, without any surprises. You can be assured of an honest approach to the service and repair work for your vehicle. We’ll only ever recommend the work your car needs.

TYRES & WHEEL ALIGNMENTS

Our team can inspect, balance, rotate and align your tyres, so you get the most out of them. When your tyres need replacing, we can fit and balance the best tyres for your vehicle and driving needs. We also can do puncture repairs for smaller punctures in your tyres.

COF CHECKS

A certificate of fitness (CoF) is a regular check to ensure that your vehicle meets the required safety standards. Vehicles requiring this certification are all passenger service vehicles such as Ubers and taxis and rental vehicles.
